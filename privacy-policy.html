Privacy Policy

Effective Date: March 2, 2026

Wobbly Flamingo (the "App") is a mobile game developed by Young Min Lee (the "Developer"). This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles user information.

1. Information We Collect

The App does not directly collect any personal information. No account creation, login, name, or email is required to use the App.

2. Locally Stored Data

The App stores the following data only on your device and does not transmit it to any external server:

  • High score record
  • Daily free play usage status

3. Third-Party Advertising (Google AdMob)

The App displays advertisements through Google AdMob. Google AdMob may automatically collect certain information for ad serving and personalization, including:

  • Advertising identifier (IDFA)
  • Device information (model, OS version)
  • IP address
  • App usage data

For more details, please refer to Google's Privacy Policy.

4. Children's Privacy

The App is suitable for all ages (rated 4+). The App does not collect any personal information from users, including children. Third-party advertising provided by Google AdMob may collect device-level data as described in Section 3. Parents or guardians can limit ad tracking through iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

The App does not store any personal data on external servers. All locally stored data is automatically removed when the App is uninstalled.

6. Your Rights

You can delete the App at any time to remove all locally stored data. To opt out of personalized advertising, go to iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking.

7. Changes to This Policy

Any changes to this Privacy Policy will be posted on this page.
