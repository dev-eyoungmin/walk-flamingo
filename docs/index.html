Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play Wobby?
Tap the screen to control your flamingo as it walks forward. The goal is to keep your balance and travel as far as possible without falling over. The controls are intentionally simple so anyone can pick it up in seconds.
Why are there ads in the game?
Wobby is free to download and play. To keep it free, the game is supported by ads provided by Google AdMob. Ads may appear between rounds or at natural breaks in gameplay. We try to keep the ad experience as non-intrusive as possible.
The game crashed or is not loading. What should I do?
First, try closing the app completely and reopening it. If the problem persists, try restarting your device. Make sure your iOS version is up to date. If you are still experiencing issues, please email us at be.onn.corp@gmail.com with a brief description of the problem and your device model.
Does Wobby collect my personal data?
Wobby uses Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect advertising identifiers (IDFA) and usage data to serve personalized ads. On iOS, the app will ask for your permission to track before any data is collected, in compliance with Apple's App Tracking Transparency (ATT) framework. You can change your preference at any time in your device's Privacy settings.
Can I play without an internet connection?
Yes. The core gameplay does not require an internet connection. Some ad content may not load when offline, but the game itself will work normally.
Is Wobby suitable for children?
Wobby is a family-friendly game with no violent content. However, because the app displays third-party ads, we recommend parental supervision for young children. Ad content is served by Google AdMob and is subject to their content policies.
How do I request a feature or give feedback?
We welcome suggestions from players. Send your ideas to be.onn.corp@gmail.com with the subject line "Feature Request" and we will review it. We cannot guarantee every request will be implemented, but all feedback is read and appreciated.

Privacy Information

Wobby is developed by an independent developer and takes your privacy seriously. Below is a plain-language summary of how data is handled:

Advertising: The app uses Google AdMob to display ads. AdMob may collect device advertising identifiers (IDFA) and general usage information to show relevant ads. This data is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

App Tracking Transparency: On iOS 14.5 and later, the app will prompt you with Apple's standard ATT dialog before any tracking occurs. You may choose to allow or decline personalized advertising. Declining will result in non-personalized ads rather than no ads.

No account required: Wobby does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information to play.

Data retention: The app itself does not store personal data on any server. Any data collected by AdMob is handled and retained according to Google's policies.

For privacy-related questions, contact us at be.onn.corp@gmail.com.
